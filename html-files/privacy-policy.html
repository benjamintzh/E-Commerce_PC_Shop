    Privacy Policy

    Last Updated: February 2025

    1. Information We Collect

    We collect personal information such as name, email, phone number, and service-related details when you engage with our services.

    If you contact us directly, we may receive additional information about you such as your name, email address, phone number, the contents of the message and/or attachments you may send us, and any other information you may choose to provide.

    When you register for an Account, we may ask for your contact information, including items such as name, company name, address, email address, and telephone number.

    2. How We Use Your Information

    Your information is used to provide and improve our services, process transactions, and communicate with you.

    We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

    • Provide, operate, and maintain our website
    • Improve, personalize, and expand our website
    • Understand and analyze how you use our website
    • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality
    • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service, to provide you with updates and other information relating to the website, and for marketing and promotional purposes
    • Send you emails

    3. Data Security

    We implement security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

    We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

    • Encryption: Sensitive information is encrypted during transmission to ensure data confidentiality.
    • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel have access to stored data, ensuring limited exposure.
    • Regular Audits & Monitoring: We conduct security audits, vulnerability assessments, and continuous monitoring to detect and mitigate threats.
    • Data Retention Policies:Your personal data is stored only as long as necessary for operational, legal, or compliance purposes. Once no longer needed, we securely dispose of or anonymize the data.
    • Third-Party Security Compliance: Any external service providers we engage with are required to meet industry-standard security and compliance protocols to protect your data.

    4. Sharing of Information

    We respect your privacy and do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. However, in certain cases, we may share your data with:

    • Trusted Third-Party Service Providers: We work with external service providers for website hosting, payment processing, analytics, customer support, and IT security. These providers only access data necessary for their services and must comply with strict data protection regulations.
    • Legal & Regulatory Authorities: We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our company, customers, or the public.
    • Business Transfers: In the event of a business merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your data may be transferred to the new entity, subject to the same privacy commitments outlined in this policy.

    We ensure that any entity receiving your data adheres to privacy and security standards in compliance with applicable laws and regulations.

    5. Your Rights

    You have the right to manage your personal data and how it is used. These rights include:

    • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
    • Correction: Modify or update your personal information if it is incorrect.
    • Deletion (Right to Be Forgotten): Request the removal of your personal data, subject to legal or operational requirements.
    • Data Portability: Obtain a copy of your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
    • Restriction of Processing: Limit how we use your data under certain circumstances.
    • Objection to Processing: Opt out of certain uses of your data, such as direct marketing.
    • Withdraw Consent: If you have given consent for data collection, you may withdraw it at any time.

    To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at contact@woutech.help.com. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe in compliance with applicable laws.

    6. Cookies & Tracking

    Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve functionality, enhance user experience, and analyze website traffic.

    Types of Cookies We Use
    • Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality (e.g., login authentication, security).
    • Performance & Analytics Cookies: Help us track site usage and improve website performance.
    • Functionality Cookies: Remember user preferences, such as language selection.
    • Advertising & Third-Party Cookies: Used for personalized marketing and retargeting by our advertising partners.
    Managing Cookies

    You have control over cookie preferences and can:

    • Adjust settings in your browser to accept, block, or delete cookies.
    • Use "Do Not Track" settings available in some browsers.
    • Opt out of third-party tracking through tools such as Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-on.

    Disabling cookies may impact certain website features, but you will still have access to basic services.
