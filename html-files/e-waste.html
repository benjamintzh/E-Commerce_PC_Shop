    What is E-Waste?

    E-waste (Electronic Waste) includes discarded electronics like computers, phones, and printers. Proper recycling prevents environmental pollution, ensures safe disposal of hazardous materials, and allows valuable components to be reused.

    Why Recycle E-Waste?

    Protect the Environment

    Proper disposal prevents harmful toxins from polluting the soil and water.

    Recover Valuable Materials

    Recycling allows valuable materials such as gold, silver, and copper to be reused.

    Reduce Landfill Waste

    Minimizes the amount of e-waste dumped in landfills, saving space and reducing hazards.

    Ensure Data Security

    Proper disposal prevents personal data from falling into the wrong hands.

    Protect Your Data Privacy

    Delete Your Accounts

    Sign out and remove accounts from your device before recycling to protect personal data.

    Factory Reset

    Perform a factory reset to erase all data and restore the device to its original settings.

    Remove SIM & Memory Cards

    Remove SIM and memory cards to safeguard stored contacts and personal files.

    Back up your data

    Physically destroy or wipe your hard drive to prevent data recovery.
